About Us

ScrubFast is a leading provider of high-quality kitchen cleaning products designed to make your daily chores easier, faster, and more effective. Founded with a passion for clean kitchens and a commitment to excellence, we've been serving households across India since 2018.

Our mission is simple: to deliver cleaning solutions that combine efficiency with environmental responsibility. We understand the challenges of maintaining a sparkling kitchen in today's busy world, and we're here to help you overcome them.

Our Mission

ScrubFast aims to deliver effective, eco-friendly, and affordable cleaning solutions for Indian households. We believe that everyone deserves a clean, hygienic kitchen without spending excessive time or money.

Through continuous innovation and a deep understanding of our customers' needs, we develop products that simplify cleaning while respecting our environment.
